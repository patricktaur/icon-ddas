About Due Diligence Automation System (DDAS)

Ver: D1.0.3 06 February, 2017, 10:50

DDAS Web Application manages creation of Investigator Compliance Search Forms for the DDAS team.

The application scans the regulatory databases and sites for information on Debarments, Regulatory Audits and Findings, Warning Letters, Notice of Disqualifications/Restrictions and Administrative Actions for the Investigators listed in the Compliance Form.

1. Perform checks on all 12 Mandatory websites and provide the result in a readable, easy to access format.

2. System searches with all the possible permutation combinations of the given search criteria (first name and last name).

3. Application records the search history and audit logs.

4. Basic reports with filters are provided.
